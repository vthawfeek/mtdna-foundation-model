The mtDNA-FM demo is live. Paste a sequence, get your haplogroup, score a variant for pathogenicity, and download your genome's 256-dim embedding. No code needed.

huggingface.co/spaces/vthawfeek/mtdna-fm-demo
---
Three tabs:
1. Haplogroup classification (26 groups, LoRA fine-tuned, top-8 confidence bar)
2. Variant pathogenicity check (ClinVar+gnomAD trained, attention heatmap at the variant position)
3. Genome embedding (256-dim, placed on reference UMAP of 100 human mtDNA genomes)
---
Engineering note: loading three models at Spaces startup reliably times out on CPU. The fix is lazy loading with a module-level cache dict. First click loads everything; every call after that is instant.
---
For the embedding UMAP: I pre-computed 2D coords for 100 reference genomes offline, then project new sequences using inverse-distance-weighted k-NN interpolation. Under 1ms, no UMAP model to serialize or load.
---
Building the first dedicated foundation model for mitochondrial DNA. mtDNA mutations cause 350+ inherited diseases and drive all maternal ancestry tracing. The model is open at github.com/vthawfeek/mtdna-foundation-model
